## Objective

Forge offers that convert without hype.

## Offer components

- **ICP**: who exactly
- **Pain**: what hurts now
- **Promise**: outcome + timeline
- **Mechanism**: why you (method/system)
- **Proof**: demos, benchmarks, testimonials, artifacts
- **Risk reversal**: refund, trial, “cancel anytime”
- **CTA**: one action

## Copy skeleton (landing)

- Headline: outcome in plain language
- Subheadline: who it’s for + why now
- Bullets: 5–7 benefits, concrete
- Proof block: screenshots/logs/case study
- Offer box: price, guarantee, CTA
- FAQ: objections
