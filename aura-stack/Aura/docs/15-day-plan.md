## Phase 1: The 15-Minute Plan (do this first)

*Set a timer for 15 minutes. Answer only what matters.*

1. **What’s the 15%?** (1–2 sentences)  
   _What’s actually left to finish?_

2. **What does “done” look like?**  
   _One clear outcome so you know when to stop._

3. **15 hours = 1 hour/day for 15 days.**  
   - Preferred time block: _______________  
   - Non‑negotiable: no more than 1 hour/day (protect the chilling).

4. **What could block you?**  
   _One thing. One simple workaround: _______________

5. **Reward after the 15 days:**  
   _One thing you’ll do to celebrate / chill._

*Stop when the timer hits 15 min. Don’t over-plan.*
