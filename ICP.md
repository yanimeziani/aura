# ICP.md

Status: Ideal collaborator profile and use-case fit.

## Primary ICP

- teams building security-sensitive, trust-aware systems
- groups requiring reproducible deployment and recovery workflows
- maintainers who value protocol contracts and clear governance

## Institutional ICP

- research institutions
- public-interest technical programs
- governance and digital infrastructure labs

## Fit Criteria

- needs deterministic operational runbooks
- values explicit threat and trust models
- can operate with HITL controls for critical actions
- can adopt OSS attribution and documentation discipline

## Non-Fit Signals

- requires opaque vendor-locked workflows
- rejects documented security/review processes
- treats protocol integrity as optional

## Sync Rule

When product scope or audience shifts, update:
- `ICP.md`
- `MARKETING.md`
- `PRD.md`
- `docs/SEED.md`
