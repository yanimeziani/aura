Voice & Tone

Do

  • Be technical and precise
  • Use sovereignty as a value
  • Speak to the infrastructure layer
  • Lead with outcomes, not features
  • Use "autonomous" not "automated"

Don't

  • Use hype ("revolutionary", "game-changing")
  • Mention specific competitors by name
  • Overpromise trading returns
  • Use passive voice
  • Shorten "infrastructure" to "infra" in copy
