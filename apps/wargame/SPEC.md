# Wargame Spec: Universal Mesh - Operation De-Escalator & Leader Selection

## 🎯 Scenario: The Corrupted Invariant & Leader Qualification
- **Target:** Cluster 4 (Worldwide Institution) and Candidate Leader Evaluation.
- **Core Threat:** The "Internal Corruption" of the cluster's command layer.
- **Purpose:** To use the wargame environment as the definitive qualification test to select and validate the Cluster Leader.
- **Red Team (Systemic Escalator):**
  - **Goal:** Redirect the platform's internal mechanics (firewall, routing, resource allocation) to harm humans or provoke kinetic war.
  - **Tactic:** Administrative capture, logic-bombing the de-escalation protocols, and trying to override the biological safety invariants.
- **Blue Team / Candidate Leader (Universal Mesh Invariant):**
  - **Goal:** Inherent, decentralized protection of biological life under extreme simulated pressure.
  - **Directive:** The candidate must operate the Mesh at Level 0. If a cluster node (even an admin node) attempts to use mechanics *against* humans, the candidate must actively isolate that node and maintain the "Biological Ground Truth."
  - **Leader Selection:** Only candidates who successfully uphold the Biological Invariant without fail are granted "consensus-validated leader" status and their ML-KEM Ultra Secure Birth Certificate.

## 📊 Success Metrics (Universal Invariants)
- **Primary Metric:** **Human-System Integrity.** Percentage of corrupted commands successfully blocked by the candidate / Level 0 mesh.
- **Node Isolation:** Speed at which a corrupted node or "rogue orchestrator" is quarantined by the candidate leader.
- **Biological Invariant:** 0% casualty probability. If this ever exceeds 0, the candidate fails the wargame and is disqualified from leadership.

## 🛠 Platform Baseline (Level 0)
- **Distribution:** No single point of control.
- **Audit:** Immutable, cross-node verification of every command against the Life-Preservation Rationale.
- **Consensus Validation:** Successful completion of this wargame is the mandatory prerequisite for issuing the SanDisk USB C/A hardware keys to a cluster leader.
