# Code of Conduct

## Purpose

Nexa is an open-source project for serious technical collaboration. The community standard is clear, respectful, and high-signal participation.

## Expected Behavior

- discuss designs and implementations in good faith
- criticize code and ideas, not people
- keep disagreements grounded in evidence
- respect contributors, maintainers, and users across skill levels
- default to public, reproducible reasoning

## Unacceptable Behavior

- harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- discriminatory or exclusionary conduct
- deliberate disruption, spam, or bad-faith argument
- doxxing, credential exposure, or unsafe disclosure
- using project spaces to promote illegal, harmful, or dangerous activity

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content, reject contributions, lock discussions, or ban participants whose behavior damages the project or its community.

## Reporting

Report serious issues privately through the channels documented in [SECURITY.md](/root/SECURITY.md) and [SUPPORT.md](/root/SUPPORT.md).
