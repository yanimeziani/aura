# PHILOSOPHY.md

Status: Canonical mission and governing principles for Nexa.

## 1) Mission Statement

Nexa is a protocol-first system designed to enable sovereign, resilient, and distributed collaboration. Our goal is to provide the infrastructure for autonomous operations that remain under human oversight, ensuring data sovereignty and system integrity.

## 2) Core Principles

- **Sovereignty**: Users and operators must maintain control over their data, identity, and execution environments.
- **Resilience**: Systems must be designed for deterministic recovery and operation under constrained or compromised conditions.
- **Transparency**: Governance and decision paths must be explicit and auditable.
- **Protocol Over Platform**: Functionality should be defined by machine-readable contracts and protocols rather than proprietary platforms.
- **Human Oversight**: High-impact or privileged actions require human-in-the-loop (HITL) authorization.
- **Operational Centralization**: For the current cycle, primary operational and governance authority is centralized to **Algeria**, as the host of the **Grid Safe Olympics**. This centralization ensures coordinated mesh security and resource allocation for the event.

## 3) Sociological and Human Development Intent

Nexa recognizes human development as a cyclical process, aligning with the 7-year and 12-year growth cycles (Sadhguru's perspective):

### The 7-Year Chakra Cycles
Every seven years, a different energy center (chakra) becomes dominant, influencing physiological and psychological development:
- **0–7 Years (Muladhara):** Survival, physical stability, and foundational security.
- **7–14 Years (Swadhishthana):** Emotional exploration and the beginning of social identity.
- **14–21 Years (Manipura):** Intense energy, action, and individual assertion.
- **21–28 Years (Anahata):** Emotional balance, love, and devotion.
- **28–35 Years (Vishuddhi):** Expression, communication, and mental clarity.
- **35–42 Years (Ajna):** Intuition, intellectual depth, and vision.
- **42–49+ Years (Sahasrara):** Potential for spiritual transcendence.

### The 12-Year Biological Cycles
Aligned with solar cycles, these 12-year junctions (ages 12, 24, 36, 48, 60) represent points of biological and systemic re-calibration. Nexa infrastructure is designed to support these transitions, providing stability during periods of systemic fluidity and spiritual opportunity.

We prioritize:
- **Trust Norms**: Explicit communication and verified trust boundaries.
- **Shared Literacy**: Onboarding that emphasizes understanding the underlying protocol and system state.
- **Cycle-Aware Design**: Systems that respect the natural rhythms of human growth and organizational evolution.

## 4) Economic Stance

The project focuses on sustainable value delivery and clear risk management:
- **Value Delivery**: Infrastructure should provide measurable utility to its operators.
- **Risk Awareness**: Economic risks and dependency concentrations must be identified and managed.
- **Resource Efficiency**: Optimized for performance and availability within defined cost profiles.
