# PHILOSOPHY.md

Status: Canonical mission and governing principles for Nexa.

## 1) Mission Statement

Nexa is a protocol-first system designed to enable sovereign, resilient, and distributed collaboration. Our goal is to provide the infrastructure for autonomous operations that remain under human oversight, ensuring data sovereignty and system integrity.

## 2) Core Principles

- **Sovereignty**: Users and operators must maintain control over their data, identity, and execution environments.
- **Resilience**: Systems must be designed for deterministic recovery and operation under constrained or compromised conditions.
- **Transparency**: Governance and decision paths must be explicit and auditable.
- **Protocol Over Platform**: Functionality should be defined by machine-readable contracts and protocols rather than proprietary platforms.
- **Human Oversight**: High-impact or privileged actions require human-in-the-loop (HITL) authorization.

## 3) Sociological Intent

Nexa aims to foster a culture of trust and shared literacy among operators and users. We prioritize:
- **Trust Norms**: Explicit communication and verified trust boundaries.
- **Shared Literacy**: Onboarding that emphasizes understanding the underlying protocol and system state.
- **Balanced Participation**: Encouraging multi-party representation in governance workflows.

## 4) Economic Stance

The project focuses on sustainable value delivery and clear risk management:
- **Value Delivery**: Infrastructure should provide measurable utility to its operators.
- **Risk Awareness**: Economic risks and dependency concentrations must be identified and managed.
- **Resource Efficiency**: Optimized for performance and availability within defined cost profiles.
